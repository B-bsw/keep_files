# Design Context (Impeccable)

- **Register:** Product
- **Users & Purpose:** Individual users managing their personal files on a dashboard focused on quick, secure, and straightforward file storage and retrieval.
- **Brand Personality:** Clean, fast, minimal (inspired by Vercel and Linear). Not an overly complex or cluttered enterprise SaaS.
- **Strategic Principles:**
  1. **Speed over ceremony:** The interface should get out of the way.
  2. **Uncompromising legibility:** High contrast, clear typography, strong visual hierarchy.
  3. **Intentional motion only:** Stable experience, purposeful motion respecting reduced-motion preferences.
