# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Individual users managing their personal files.

## Product Purpose
A personal file management dashboard focused on quick, secure, and straightforward file storage and retrieval.

## Brand Personality
Clean, fast, minimal (inspired by Vercel and Linear).

## Anti-references
Not an overly complex or cluttered enterprise SaaS. Should avoid generic templates and unnecessary ornamentation.

## Design Principles
- **Speed over ceremony:** The interface should get out of the way, allowing users to interact with their files instantly.
- **Uncompromising legibility:** High contrast, clear typography, and strong visual hierarchy must guide the user without confusion.
- **Intentional motion only:** Prioritize a stable experience. Any motion should be purposeful and respect reduced-motion preferences.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strong focus on reduced motion and clear legibility (high contrast, distinct interactive elements, and robust semantic structure).
